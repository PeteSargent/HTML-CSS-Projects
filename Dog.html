Inugami Korone, The Dog-girl Virtual Youtuber

Inugami Korone (戌神ころね) is a female Japanese Virtual YouTuber associated with Hololive,
debuting as part of the group "hololive Gamers" (ホロライブゲーマーズ) alongside Shirakami Fubuki,
Ookami Mio and Nekomata Okayu.

Nick Names
  • Koro-san (by Nekomata Okayu)
  • Koronen
  • Doggo (and variants)
  • DOOG
  • God Dog
  • Korogi-senpai (by Amane Kanata)
  • Inugami Kotaro (host club persona)
